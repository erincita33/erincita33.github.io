Private Swimming Lessons with Erin Wooldridge

Choose an experienced instructor to travel to your home in the Atherton/Menlo area.

 

I teach children and adults at every level of experience and comfort. My students range from 6 months old to adults in all stages of life. I have come to know by experience that everybody can swim.

  

I love teaching children with fear or special challenges. I also love teaching adults who have never learned how to swim and have a lot to conquer. For me, every student is "special needs" because I teach to their unique and individual needs and according to what works best. I am totally focused on their success and I know when to be patient and when to push for progress.

 

Call or email today and we can work out a plan that best fits your needs. We will talk about goals and logistics and that will help us work out the frequency and duration of your lessons.
