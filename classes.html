Classes

I teach private and semi-private lessons.  I highly recommend private lessons for students with fear, great difficulty with paying attention, and children under the age of 6.

 

Some examples of skills you can learn or improve:

 

Rhythmic breathing- blowing mouth and nose bubbles underwater and breathing through your mouth when you come up

 

Buoyancy- finding your perfect float, face down and on your back


Returning to the wall- (this is especially important for young children who may fall in the pool one day or jump when no one is there to help)

 

Streamlining- learning to propel through the water without fighting it, gracefully and smoothly

 

Different types of kicks- flutter, whip kick, dolphin kick

 

Competitive Strokes- Freestyle, Backstroke, Breaststroke, and Butterfly

 

Treading water- keeping your head above deep water using a combination of buoyancy, kicking, and sculling water with your arms
